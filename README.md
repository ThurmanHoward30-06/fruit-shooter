# 🍓 FRUIT SHOOTER 🍎

A fun, fast-paced fruit shooting game! Shoot falling fruits before they hit the ground and survive increasingly difficult waves.

## 🎮 Play Now

**Play Online:** https://thurmanhoward30-06.github.io/fruit-shooter/

## 🎯 How to Play

- **Move Mouse/Touch** - Aim at falling fruits
- **Click/Tap & Hold** - Continuous shooting
- **Destroy Fruits** - Earn points and advance waves
- **Survive** - Don't let any fruit hit the ground!

## 🏆 Features

✅ Fast-paced gameplay  
✅ Progressive wave system  
✅ Leaderboard tracking  
✅ Mobile & desktop support  
✅ Beautiful retro UI  
✅ Free to play  

## 📊 Scoring

- 10 points per fruit destroyed
- Waves increase every 15 fruits
- Fruit speed increases with each wave

## 💾 Leaderboard

Your scores are saved locally. Check the leaderboard to see top players!
